Easy add social buttons in menu bar...

== Description ==
This plugin was made to easy fill up the empty space in menu bar with few off most popular social network icons... 
Hope that it will useful to you...

There are 20 icons to choose from: Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, Pinterest, Google +, LinkedIn, Behance, Blogger, DeviantArt, Dribbble, Flickr, Last.fm, MySpace, RSS, Skype, Tumblr, Vimeo, VKontakte, Wordpress and YouTube.Step by step instructions

1) Go to Apperance - Menus.

2) In the top right corner of the page click on SCREEN OPTIONS.

3) Than click on SOCIAL NETWORKS.

4) Than you must find Social networks on the bottom of a Menu Structure on the left side of a page and click on it.

5) Click on Social menu and than click on Add to Menu button.

6) Click on Save menu button.On Settings page you can choose which social networks icons to show on your menu.
